Privacy Policy for the Mailing List

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you sign up to receive email updates from us. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

What personal data do we collect?

We collect your email address when you sign up to receive emails from us. We do not collect any other personal data from you within the context of the mailing list.

How do we use your personal data?

We use your email address to send you emails about our project, according to the explicit consent given by you. We will not use your email address for any other purpose.

How long do we retain your personal data?

We will retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed to our mailing list. If your email bounces back, we will remove your email address from our mailing list. You can unsubscribe from our mailing list at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email or by using the built-in unsubscribe feature of your mail client.

How do we protect your personal data?

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to ensure that your personal data is secure and protected against unauthorised access, alteration, or destruction. Your personal data is stored on a secured server within Germany and is not accessible to any third parties.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You also have the right to withdraw your consent at any time, or to object to our processing of your personal data. You may also request to be blacklisted from ever being subscribed again. To exercise any of these rights, please visit the unsubscribe page linked in every email or contact the site owner at the contact information provided below.

Transferral of personal data

We reserve the right to transfer the personal data and the consent given by you to the person "Seneral" to a future company or similar legal entity, so long as it is majorly held and operated by the person "Seneral". Under no circumstance shall the personal data be exposed to another third party during this process, and the server storing your personal data will not change during this process.

Data Protection Contact

Data Protection is handled by the site owner, Seneral. If you have any questions or concerns about how we collect, use, or protect your personal data, you can contact Seneral at the following email address: contact@seneral.dev.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time. Future significant expansions to this privacy policy will not inflict on the privacy of the personal data collected now solely in the context of the mailing list.
