About Me

My name is Seneral, aka Seneral, and I am a german student at the age of 16.
I'm also a passionate Unity developer and especially love programming it's editor.
My greatest fascination about developmenet is (interactive) procedural/parametric modeling.

Alt1

My hobby began with 3D-modeling in Blender, which I put to practice for the TESRenewal: Skywind project in 2014.
Over the Blender Game Engine I started developing in Unity in 2013 and quickly focused on editor tools.
I published my first project in March 2015 which has now become the popular opensource Node Editor Framework.
My projects range from small IMGUI hacks over character and mesh generation tools to a GPU Erosion Simulation and TC2 Painter.

Alt1

I first started exploring the world of game developement over 4 years ago, starting with 3D-modeling in Blender.
I then somehow quickly found my way over the Blender Game Engine to Unity, where I now develop for about 3years.
I'm a proud member of the Unity community and love contributing and interacting with it.

The first row of terrain tools, accompanying Terrain Composer 2, are on the way to be released though, like TC2 Node Painter and Advanced GPU Erosion Simulation.
